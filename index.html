ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Hi! My name is Frankie, and I really like music. So much so that I've decided to create an entire website dedicated to three of my favorite artists (and more).

I've picked three musicians/bands that are wildly different from one another to showcase how big and beautiful the world of music really is.
Each page will delve into the history of the artists and tell you exactly where you should start listening, if you're new to their discographies.

So! Pick one and get started!
